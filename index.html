Finland
Finland is the ultimate escape for the soul. Indulge in the cornerstone of Finnish culture—the traditional sauna—for deep relaxation and rejuvenation. Explore stylish, modern cities like Helsinki, then venture north to meet Santa Claus in Rovaniemi. Whether you're dog-sledding or foraging for berries, Finland is defined by its serene, honest luxury and design.

Iceland
Your Icelandic adventure is an encounter with the elements. Dive into the soothing, mineral-rich waters of the Blue Lagoon, hike across ancient lava fields, or explore crystal ice caves carved into vast glaciers. In winter, chase the dazzling Northern Lights across a star-filled sky; in summer, enjoy the endless light of the midnight sun. It is pure, exhilarating nature.

Get ready for a sun-drenched, exhilarating escape.

Netherland
Embrace the effortless Dutch lifestyle by getting on a bike. With miles of flat, dedicated cycling paths, the bicycle is your perfect tool for discovery. Explore quaint villages, cycle past serene polders, and connect with nature at a relaxed pace. From the world-class museums of The Hague to the lively markets of Rotterdam, the Netherlands is a joy to navigate.

Ireland
Step into the legendary landscapes of Ireland, the Emerald Isle. Discover a land where dramatic coastal cliffs, like the magnificent Cliffs of Moher, plunge into the Atlantic, and rolling green hills are dotted with ancient ruins and castles. Here, the raw beauty of the wilderness and a thousand years of history greet you at every turn.
